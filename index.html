NFC Potentiostat – Battery-Free Electrochemical Platform

BME4496 · Hardware + Firmware + Android + On-Device AI

A fully wireless, battery-free potentiostat powered entirely by NFC energy harvesting from a smartphone. Custom 4-layer PCB (STM32U545 + ST25DV), 3D-printed magnetic enclosure, CNC-milled 13.56 MHz antennas, and a companion Android app with real-time plotting and an on-device Gemma 3 1B AI agent for voice-controlled analysis. TÜBİTAK 2209-B approved.

Challenge: Benchtop potentiostats are bulky, expensive, and need wired power — unusable for portable point-of-care diagnostics.
Solution: Designed the full stack end-to-end (analog AFE, embedded firmware over an ST25DV mailbox protocol, Android app, fine-tuned on-device AI), cutting cost >90% vs. lab equipment with 98.5% measured accuracy.
NanoBiosens Lab — real-time cyclic voltammetry in the companion app
